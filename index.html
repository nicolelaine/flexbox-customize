Hi, I'm Nicole!

I'm a map lover, maker, and doer, who currently lives in Hamburg, Germany. Take a look at some projects that I've been working on!

Maptime Berlin is quarterly event of map enthusiasts and mapping professionals alike. We offer workshops, have interesting lectures, and we occasionally go on an outting to a map library or exhibtion.

Missing Maps is an OpenStreetMap project that works in conjuction with the Red Cross to help put vulnerable communities on the map. We help map communties that have experienced or will soon experience hurricanes, floods, or other natural disasters.

"Map your Desire Path" is a collaborate project to put the Desire Paths you see in the world around you onto the map. Desire Paths can show where the people "voted with their feet" and can be helpful in planning future cities.
